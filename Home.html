The Backrooms

A Collaborative Field Guide to the Spaces Between

If you're not careful and you noclip out of reality in the wrong areas, you'll end up in the Backrooms — a non-Euclidean expanse of flickering fluorescent lights, aged wallpaper, and the constant hum of something you can never quite locate. This archive documents what has been found — and what has been lost — within.

⚠   Enter at your own risk. Reality is unstable beyond this point.

// Field Notes

📋   Survival Basics

  • Do not run unless you know what is behind you.
  • Almond Water restores sanity — ration it carefully.
  • Fluorescent buzzing does not always mean safety.
  • If a level feels familiar, it probably isn't.
  • Not all exits lead forward. Some lead deeper.

📖   About This Archive

This wiki is a collaborative record maintained by wanderers, explorers, and survivors who have passed through the Backrooms and returned with documentation. Entries are verified where possible. Some information may be incomplete, contradictory, or deliberately redacted for the safety of future readers.
